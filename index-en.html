Account Deletion Request

Welcome to the account deletion page for Inter cOOK.

Account Deletion Procedure

To delete your account and associated data, please follow these steps:

  1. Send an email to our support team at intercook2024@gmail.com with the email address associated with your account.
  2. Our team will confirm your deletion request within 48 hours.
  3. Once your request is confirmed, your account and all associated data will be deleted within 14 business days.

Data Deleted or Retained

The following data will be deleted:

  • Profile data (name, email address, etc.).
  • Activity history and user preferences.

Please note that some data may be temporarily retained to comply with legal obligations (such as billing data) before permanent deletion.

Retention Period

Data is retained up to 14 days after your request is confirmed. After this period, all information will be permanently deleted.

For further questions, please contact us at intercook2024@gmail.com.
